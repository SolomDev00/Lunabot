Everyone

!help [command name]

Shows the help menu.

Aliases: h

!play [song name/url]

Plays a song.

Aliases: p

!pause

Pauses the current playing song.

Aliases: pu, break

!resume

Resumes the current paused song.

Aliases: r, continue

!queue

Shows the queue.

Aliases: q, list

!skip

Skip to the next song or any song in queue.

Aliases: s, next

!playskip [song name/url]

Adds a music to the queue and the skips to this music.

Aliases: ps

!playtop [song name/url]

Adds a music in the top of the queue.

Aliases: pt

!back

Back to the previous song.

Aliases: b

!volume [1-200]

Change the volume.

Aliases: vol

!autoplay [on/off]

Toggle the bot to continuously queue up recommended tracks.

Aliases: ap, auto

!replay

Replay the current song.

Aliases: rp

!nowplaying

Displays info about the song.

Aliases: np, si, song, songinfo

!addprevious

Add previous song to the queue.

Aliases: addback

!filters

To view all filters.

Aliases: fl

!lyrics

Gets the lyrics of the current playing song.

Aliases: lyric, ly

!clear

Remove the queue and stop the song.

Aliases: c, empty

!stop

Stops the player and clear the queue.

Aliases: dc

!clean

Clean bot message and any message related to the bot commands.

!ping

Get the bot latency.

Aliases: latency

!join

Joins the Bot in your Channel.

!loop

Repeat the queue or the song.

Aliases: l, repeat

!loop queue

Loop the queue.

Aliases: loop q

!loop song

Loop the current playing song.

Aliases: loop song

!loop off

Turn looping off.

Aliases: loop f

!shuffle

Shuffles the queue.

Aliases: sh

!move [song number]

Move a song to the top of the queue or to specific position.

Aliases: mv

!move [from][to]

Move the selected song to the provided position.

!moveme

Moves you to the bot, if playing something.

Aliases: mm

!remove [song number]

Remove a specific song from the queue.

Aliases: rm, del, delete

!leave

Leave the channel & stops the song.

Aliases: dc, disconnect

!search

Search in Youtube.

Aliases: yts, youtube

!forward [time]

Fast forward the player by your specified amount; The default 10 seconds.

forward 100 - Forwards 100 milliseconds into the song.

Aliases: ff

!rewind [time]

Rewinds the player by your specified amount; The default is 10 seconds.

rewind 100 - Rewinds 100 milliseconds back into the song.

Aliases: rw

!seek mm:ss

Change the position of the track or to specific time.

seek 1:00 make the song start from 1 min.
seek 5 make the song start from 5 sec.
seek +70 make the song skip 70 sec.
seek -70 make the song back 70 sec.
